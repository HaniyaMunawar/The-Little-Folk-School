About Us

Shaping bright futures since 1953.

Education

A place where children could receive quality education in a nurturing environment. His dedication continues to inspire the school's journey and legacy today.

Little Folk’s School was established in 1953 in North Nazimabad, Karachi, at a time when the area was still a remote suburb. Over the decades, the school has grown into a well-known name in education, with multiple branches across the city.

The school's mission has always been to provide quality education, build character, and encourage creativity in every child. Our focus on values, discipline, and modern teaching methods makes us a trusted choice for parents.

Vision & Mission

Our vision is to become a leading educational institution that nurtures bright, ethical, and confident future leaders.

Our mission is to create a safe and inspiring learning environment where students receive a balanced education — academic, physical, emotional, and moral — that prepares them for success in life.
