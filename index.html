About Us

Little Folk’s School is one of the oldest schools in Karachi, started in 1953 in North Nazimabad. We now has several branches in the city, including a Cambridge section in Gulistan-e-Jauhar. Our school offers both the local SSC and Cambridge O-Level systems. We are known for good education and has facilities like science labs, libraries, sports areas, and indoor games. With years of experience, we remains a trusted school for many families.
